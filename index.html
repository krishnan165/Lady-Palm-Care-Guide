Lady Palm Care Guide

Soil for Lady Palm

Well-draining, rich potting mix.

Lady Palms prefer a well-draining soil mix with added organic matter for optimal growth.

Watering

Moderate watering, keeping the soil evenly moist.

Water the plant moderately, maintaining consistent moisture without allowing the soil to become waterlogged.

Lighting

Filtered, indirect light.

Place the Lady Palm in a location with filtered, indirect light; it can tolerate lower light conditions but avoid direct sunlight.

Benefits

Graceful, arching fronds.

Lady Palms are known for their elegant, arching fronds that add a touch of sophistication to indoor spaces.

Effective air purifier.

Lady Palms help improve indoor air quality by removing common pollutants.

Non-Benefits

May be sensitive to overwatering; avoid waterlogged soil.
